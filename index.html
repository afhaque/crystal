Crystal Collector!

A game of guesstimations. Simple now, addicting later.



Instructions

Below, you will see four crystals, a random number to which you will match, and your point box. In order to win the game, you must click the crystals in a way in which causes your total points, to match the random number given to you at the start of each round. The values behind each crystal differ from one another, and can only be discovered through clicking each crystal. The trick is, after you win (or lose) a game, the values behind each crystal change, as well as the points you are trying to match. If your total points yields a number as small as one point higher than the given number, you will lose. Get those calculators ready, and good luck!
